How to Claim a Lost Item

1
Search the Registry
Use the search bar and tag filters above to find your lost item by color, category, or location.
2
Visit DRRMO Office
Bring a valid school ID to the Lost & Found office — Admin Building, Room 104. Open Mon–Fri, 7 AM–6 PM.
3
Claim Your Item
Present proof of ownership. The officer will verify, release the item, and log it in the claim register.
